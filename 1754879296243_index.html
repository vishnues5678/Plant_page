Our Story

Flora was born from a simple idea: to share the joy of living with plants. We believe that bringing a bit of nature into your home can transform your space and your well-being.

Founded in 2020, our passion for greenery has grown into a curated collection of the finest houseplants, sourced from the best local growers. We're dedicated to providing healthy, beautiful plants and the knowledge you need to help them thrive.

Thank you for joining us on this green journey. We're excited to help you find your perfect plant companion.
